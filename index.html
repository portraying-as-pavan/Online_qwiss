About Us

Q-WISSENAIRE is a National Level Competitive Exam for students of 8th, 9th,10th, +1 and +2 to bring about a qualitative improvement of education in the country.

This examination provide an opportunity for JEE Aspirants to compare and compete with other peers across country. We seek to provide the students with a platform to interact with the students of an IIT, and to build fundamental experience and knowledge by thinking out of the box.

Examination Pattern

DURATION

2 hours examination for students of 8th, 9th & 10th
3 hours examination for students of +1 and +2.


DATE & VENUE

2nd week of December 2019
At your Respective School or College.


EXAMINATION FEE

Rs.100 per student for 8th, 9th& 10th
Rs.150 per student for +1 and +2.


EXAMINATION MODEL

40 questions from Mathematics, Reasoning and Mental Ability for classes 8th, 9th& 10th
40 questions from Mathematics, Physics and Chemistry for classes +1 and +2
Questions will be prepared by professors of IIT Bhubaneswar

Prizes and Benefits

• Certificate of Merit from Wissenaire, IIT Bhubaneswar for the year-wise toppers from each college.
